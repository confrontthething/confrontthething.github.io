You’ve been running away from The Thing all your life, but a piece of it has managed to invade your safe place. With the help of your old friend, Frinchfry, you set out to fight the infiltrator. Will you shut it out for good or finally choose to confront it?
The Thing I Don’t Want to Think About is a narrative adventure game that we created as part of the 3D Game Development Capstone course at The University of Texas at Austin.
